Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email, and profile picture. When you play, we store your scores, achievements, and game statistics. When you submit content, we store your photos and any promotional information you provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the game experience
  • To display leaderboards and achievements
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To moderate user-submitted content
  • To display promotional content you submit

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers. Photos are stored on Supabase Storage. Payment data is handled by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google — Authentication
  • Supabase — Database and storage
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Vercel — Hosting
  • ESRI — Satellite map imagery

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store game progress, preferences, and session data. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Children

PinTheWorld is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

9. Contact

Questions? Contact us at support@pintheworld.online
